Associated Projects

The United States PostgreSQL Associated has created an Associated Project framework to give legal and financial support to projects that are aligned with PgUS's mission for their individual goals. If your project aligns with the PgUS mission and you wish your project to be associated with PgUS, please review the Associated Project guidelines and contact us at secretary@postgresql.us.



For more information on how we're involved with these associated projects, please visit our associated projects page.

  • PGConf US
  • PostgresOpen
