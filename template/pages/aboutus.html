about us

mission statement

The US PostgreSQL Association is a non profit public charity with the following purpose:

(a) Educate, promote and support the creation, development and use of the PostgreSQL Open Source Database software, a software system which is available to the general public without charge;

(b) Provide information and education regarding the use of PostgreSQL; and

(c) Organize, hold and conduct meetings, discussion, and forums on the contemporary issues concerning the use of PostgreSQL.

Here is a copy of our bylaws, and our IRS determination letter. For more information, please visit our corporate page. Information on our full board of directors can be found here.

how can I contribute?

If you are interested in supporting us, the best way is to become a member and actively contribute to our mission.

However, donations are also an excellent way to express appreciation for what we do.
